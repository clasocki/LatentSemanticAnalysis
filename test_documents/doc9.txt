Mr Trump has won in 12 states, out of a total of 20 primaries. Mr Cruz has won six, including his home state of Texas, the largest state to vote to date. Mr Rubio has so far won just one state.
With Mr Trump looking the most likely candidate to gain the Republican nomination, winning in states nationwide, the billionnaire businessman called himself a "unifier".
The Republican establishment has been left wondering how the outsider - who was not considered a credible candidate when he first announced his candidacy - can be handled.
